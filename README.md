AutoMark AI is an AI-powered automated marketing content generator and social media scheduler for small businesses, startups, and NGOs.

## Problem
Small organizations struggle to maintain a consistent digital presence due to limited time, marketing skills, and resources.

## Solution
The platform analyzes a business website to understand brand identity and services. It then generates social media captions, posters, and short videos and schedules posts across platforms with user approval.

## AI Implementation Note
This project is a functional prototype demonstrating the complete AI-driven workflow.
AI modules such as website analysis and content generation are currently simulated.
Real AI models will be integrated in future development.

## Key Features
- Website analysis
- AI-generated marketing content
- Social media scheduling
- User approval workflow
