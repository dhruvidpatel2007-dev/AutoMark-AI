AutoMark AI is an AI-powered marketing automation platform for small businesses, startups, and NGOs.

## Problem
Small organizations struggle to maintain a consistent digital presence due to limited time and marketing resources.

## Solution
AutoMark AI analyzes a business website to understand brand identity and automatically generates social media content. It also schedules and posts content with user approval.

## Features
- Website analysis
- AI-generated captions and creatives
- Social media scheduling
- Performance analytics
